Omek Houria Menu

Appetizers

  • Brik - A thin pastry filled with tuna, egg, and parsley, then fried until crispy.
  • Mechouia - Grilled vegetable salad with tomatoes, peppers, and onions, seasoned with olive oil and spices.
  • Chakchouka - A flavorful dish made with tomatoes, peppers, onions, and eggs, seasoned with spices and herbs.

Main Courses

  • Couscous - Steamed semolina served with a rich stew of lamb, vegetables, and chickpeas.
  • Kafteji - A dish made from mashed vegetables such as potatoes, peppers, and tomatoes, mixed with olive oil and spices.
  • Mloukhia - A hearty stew made from jute leaves, cooked with chicken or lamb and served with rice.

Desserts

  • Baklava - Layers of phyllo pastry filled with chopped nuts and sweetened with honey or syrup.
  • Makroudh - Deep-fried semolina pastry filled with dates and flavored with orange blossom water.
  • Bambalouni - Tunisian doughnuts, deep-fried and dusted with powdered sugar.
